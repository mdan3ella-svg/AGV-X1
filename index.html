AVG-X1
ENGINEERING

The AVG-X1 represents the pinnacle of anti-gravity transport, blending structural integrity with fluid propulsion aesthetics. Designed for the industrial future, it optimizes weight-to-thrust ratios.

Vehicle Specifications

The AVG-X1 employs a modular gravity-repulsion core that operates at 99.9% energy efficiency. The vehicle length is 4.5m with a variable width chassis that adjusts to atmospheric density.

Advanced Materials

Graphene-Carbon Matrix: Provides a 40% reduction in weight compared to standard titanium alloys. Piezoelectric Skin: Harvests ambient energy from atmospheric drag to supplement main power cells.

Primary Components

Repulsion Core: The central gravitational engine. Dynamic Winglets: Micro-actuated control surfaces for high-speed maneuverability. Energy Buffer: A high-capacity super-capacitor array for instant peak output.
